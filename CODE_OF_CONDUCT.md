# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The **Ocean Floor Research Data Management Toolkit** is a community resource that supports collaboration, knowledge sharing, and open science.

We are committed to fostering a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for everyone who participates in this project, regardless of their background, identity, experience, or role.

We encourage constructive discussions, respectful feedback, and collaborative problem-solving.

This repository supports the principles of **FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable)** and **CARE (Collective Benefit, Authority to Control, Responsibility, and Ethics)** data principles, as well as responsible research data management and open science. We encourage collaboration that advances these principles while respecting diverse perspectives, knowledge systems, and the rights and interests of all communities connected to research data.

---

## Expected Behaviour

We ask all contributors and participants to:

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Welcome different perspectives and experiences.
- Provide constructive feedback and suggestions.
- Focus discussions on improving the repository and supporting the research community.
- Respect intellectual property, licensing, and attribution.
- Help create a positive and inclusive environment for all users.

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

Examples of unacceptable behaviour include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Offensive or inappropriate language.
- Intimidation or deliberate disruption of discussions.
- Publishing private or confidential information without permission.
- Any behaviour that would make others feel unwelcome or unsafe.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions related to this repository, including:

- GitHub Issues
- Pull Requests
- Discussions
- Project communications related to this repository

---

## Reporting Concerns

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact the **Ocean Floor Data Stewardship Team**.

You may:

- Open a private communication with one of the repository maintainers, or
- Contact the Data Stewardship Team through the Ocean Floor project.

All reports will be treated respectfully and, where appropriate, confidentially.

---

## Enforcement

The repository maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
